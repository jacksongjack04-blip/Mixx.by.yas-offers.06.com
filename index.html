What is Mixx by Yas?
Mixx by Yas is a mobile financial services platform that allows you to manage your money, send and receive payments, buy airtime, pay bills, and access a wide range of financial services directly from your mobile phone.
How do I sign up for Mixx?
To sign up, simply download the Mixx by Yas app from the Google Play Store or Apple App Store, or visit the nearest Yas store. You'll need a valid mobile number and a valid identification document.
What services does Mixx offer?
Mixx offers a wide range of services including money transfer, airtime purchase, bill payments (electricity, water, TV), merchant payments, savings, loans, insurance, and more.
Is Mixx by Yas secure?
Yes, Mixx by Yas uses industry-standard security protocols including encryption, PIN protection, and two-factor authentication to ensure your financial transactions and personal information are safe and secure.
How do I earn Mixx Points?
You earn Mixx Points every time you use Mixx services - whether you send money, buy airtime, pay bills, or make purchases. The more you transact, the more points you earn. Points can be redeemed for airtime, SMS, cash, and other rewards.
How do I contact customer support?
You can reach our customer support team through the Mixx app's help section, by calling our support hotline, visiting any Yas store, or using the assistance feature on our website. Our team is available 24/7 to help you with any issues.

Yas is part of the Axian Telecom Group, a leading telecommunications company dedicated to connecting people and businesses in Tanzania with innovative solutions.
